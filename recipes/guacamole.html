Guacamole

Description

Introducing the one and only guacamole, the green goodness that will make your taste buds dance and your cravings soar. With its creamy texture, zesty lime, and vibrant blend of spices, this delectable dip is a fiesta in every bite. So grab your chips, gather your amigos, and prepare for a guacamole extravaganza that'll leave you shouting, '¡Olé!'

Let's dive into the irresistible allure of guacamole, where every scoop brings a burst of avocado bliss and takes your palate on a journey to pure culinary paradise.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • ½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.
  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor
